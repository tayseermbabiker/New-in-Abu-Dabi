Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to send cargo from Abu Dhabi?

Air cargo: AED 15-45/kg (3-7 days). Sea cargo: AED 100-150/box (6-12 weeks). LBC balikbayan box to Philippines: AED 115/box (sea, 8-10 weeks). Al Madina cargo to India/Pakistan: AED 12-18/kg (sea) or AED 28-42/kg (air). Door-to-door delivery available with most services. Weight restrictions: Max 30kg/box for sea, up to 100kg for air depending on destination.

What items are prohibited in international cargo shipping from Abu Dhabi?

Prohibited items include: Liquids (perfumes, oils, beverages), flammable materials, batteries, sharp objects, perishable food, medicines, live plants, and alcohol. Allowed items: Clothing, dry food (sealed), toys, electronics (without batteries), books, and household items. Check destination country customs restrictions as some countries ban specific foods or products. Always declare electronics and valuables.

Which are the best shipping companies in Abu Dhabi for international cargo?

The top cargo shipping companies in Abu Dhabi include: LBC Express (best for Philippines balikbayan boxes, rated 4.4 with 500+ reviews), Al Madina Cargo on Electra Street (best for India and Pakistan, rated 4.3), and GAC Logistics at KIZAD and Mina Zayed (best for global shipments to 50+ countries, rated 4.4). LBC specializes in sea and air cargo to Manila, while Al Madina covers both major cities and small towns across the subcontinent.

How long does international shipping take from Abu Dhabi?

Shipping times from Abu Dhabi vary by method and destination. Air cargo typically takes 3-7 days for most destinations. Sea cargo to the Philippines takes 8-10 weeks, to India and Pakistan 6-8 weeks, and to other destinations 6-12 weeks. Sea shipping is 60-70% cheaper than air but significantly slower. During peak seasons like Christmas and Eid, expect delays of 1-2 additional weeks. Door-to-door delivery is available with most cargo companies for both air and sea shipments.

What are the international shipping rates from Abu Dhabi in 2026?

International shipping rates from Abu Dhabi in 2026: Air cargo ranges from AED 15-45 per kg depending on destination and urgency. Sea cargo starts at AED 12/kg for the Indian subcontinent and AED 115 per balikbayan box to the Philippines. GAC Logistics offers custom quotes for worldwide shipments via air, sea and land. To save money, consolidate shipments with friends, avoid peak seasons, and choose sea cargo when timing is flexible. Insurance is recommended for shipments valued over AED 2,000.
