Frequently Asked Questions About Cargo Shipping from Abu Dhabi

How much does it cost to send cargo from Abu Dhabi?

Air cargo: AED 15-45/kg (3-7 days). Sea cargo: AED 100-150/box (6-12 weeks). LBC balikbayan box to Philippines: AED 115/box (sea, 8-10 weeks). Alfares cargo to India/Pakistan: AED 20-30/kg (air). Door-to-door delivery available with most services. Weight restrictions: Max 30kg/box for sea, up to 100kg for air depending on destination.

What items are prohibited in international cargo shipping?

Prohibited items: Liquids (perfumes, oils, beverages), flammable materials, batteries, sharp objects, perishable food, medicines, live plants, alcohol. Allowed: Clothing, dry food (sealed), toys, electronics (without batteries), books, household items. Check destination country customs restrictions - some countries ban specific foods or products. Always declare electronics and valuables.
