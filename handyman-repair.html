What's Included vs Extra Costs

Usually Included in Hourly Rate

  • Basic hand tools (screwdriver, hammer, pliers)
  • Small hardware (screws, nails, basic fittings)
  • Transportation to your location
  • Basic consultation and assessment

Extra Costs to Expect

  • Materials and Parts: Cost price + 10-20% service fee
  • Specialized Tools: Drill bits, saw blades, specific fixtures
  • Major Components: Light fixtures, faucets, electrical switches
  • Paint and Supplies: If painting work required
  • Disposal Fees: Removing old fixtures or materials

Sample Project Costs

  • Hang 3 Pictures: AED 80-120 total
  • Mount 50-inch TV: AED 200-350 total
  • Install Bathroom Shelf: AED 100-150 total
  • Fix Leaky Faucet: AED 120-200 total
  • Assemble IKEA Wardrobe: AED 150-250 total

Money-Saving Tips & Shortcuts

Booking Tips

  • Book multiple tasks together -- better hourly rates
  • Avoid weekends and evenings -- premium pricing
  • Buy materials yourself -- avoid markup fees
  • Use first-time customer discounts -- many apps offer them
  • Get quotes from 3 providers -- compare total project cost

Time-Saving Tips

  • Early morning visits: Less crowded, faster service
  • Take photos of the problem or project
  • List everything you need done for bulk pricing
  • Check reviews and ratings carefully

Remember: Always confirm rates and availability directly with service providers before booking. Keep digital and physical copies of all receipts and work documentation.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do handyman services cost in Abu Dhabi?

Handyman services in Abu Dhabi range from AED 50-150 per hour depending on the provider type. Budget options through Facebook freelance groups cost AED 50-80/hour. Mid-range services like SmileHandyy and ServiceMarket charge AED 89-129/hour. Premium app-based services such as Urban Company and Mr Usta run AED 120-150/hour. All prices exclude materials and parts, which are charged separately.

What is the minimum booking for a handyman in Abu Dhabi?

Minimum booking varies by provider. Budget freelancers typically require 1-2 hours minimum. Mid-range services like SmileHandyy have a 1-hour minimum at AED 99 or 3+ hours at AED 89/hour. ServiceMarket requires a 2-hour minimum at AED 129/hour and sends two handymen per booking. Premium services generally require 2-4 hours minimum. Emergency same-day bookings have a 1-hour minimum with a 20-30% surcharge.

Can I get same-day emergency handyman service in Abu Dhabi?

Yes, several providers offer same-day emergency handyman services in Abu Dhabi. OnTimeFix specializes in quick same-day repairs for electrical, plumbing, and AC emergencies. SmileHandyy offers same-day availability with 24-hour notice preferred. ServiceMarket and Urban Company also have same-day booking options. Expect a 20-30% surcharge on standard hourly rates for emergency and after-hours service, with a 1-hour minimum booking.

What is included in a handyman's hourly rate in Abu Dhabi?

Most handyman hourly rates in Abu Dhabi include basic hand tools (screwdriver, hammer, pliers), small hardware (screws, nails, basic fittings), transportation to your location, and a basic consultation. Extra costs include materials and parts (cost price plus 10-20% service fee), specialized tools, major components like light fixtures and faucets, paint, and disposal fees. For example, hanging 3 pictures costs AED 80-120 total, mounting a 50-inch TV costs AED 200-350, and assembling an IKEA wardrobe costs AED 150-250.
