Frequently Asked Questions About Nurseries in Abu Dhabi

What is the best preschool in Abu Dhabi?

Top-rated nurseries: Redwood Montessori (4.7‚òÖ, AED 65,000-85,000/year, multilingual), Discovery Nursery (4.5‚òÖ, AED 45,000-60,000/year, British Foundation Stage), Falcon British Nursery (4.4‚òÖ, AED 25,000-40,000/year, ADEK approved). Redwood offers English, Arabic, and French. Discovery focuses on play-based learning. Falcon is most affordable with transport and meals included.

How much does nursery cost in Abu Dhabi?

Nursery fees range: Budget options AED 18,000-40,000/year (Falcon British, Little Steps Mussafah, Smart Kids MBZ), Mid-range AED 45,000-65,000/year (Discovery Nursery), Premium AED 65,000-85,000/year (Redwood Montessori). Most nurseries accept payment in 3 installments (September, January, April). Additional costs: Registration fee AED 500-2,000, uniform AED 200-500, books/materials AED 300-800/year.

When should I apply for nursery in Abu Dhabi?

Best time: February-April for September start (peak admission season). Popular nurseries fill up quickly - apply 6 months before your desired start date. Wait lists are common, so apply to 2-3 nurseries. Visit during operational hours to see facilities, meet teachers, and observe classes. Most nurseries offer tours on weekdays 9 AM-12 PM. Bring required documents: passport copies, visa, Emirates ID, vaccination records.

What is the difference between British and Montessori nurseries?

British nurseries (Discovery, Falcon): Follow Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) curriculum, structured play-based learning, prepare for British primary schools. Montessori nurseries (Redwood): Child-led learning, hands-on activities, mixed age groups, focus on independence and self-directed exploration. British better for: Families planning UK/British school pathway, structured routines. Montessori better for: Encouraging independence, multilingual education, child-paced learning.

Are there affordable nurseries in Abu Dhabi under AED 40,000?

Yes! Budget-friendly options: Falcon British Nursery (AED 25,000-40,000, Khalifa City A, transport + meals included), Little Steps Nursery (AED 18,000-28,000, Mussafah), Smart Kids Nursery (AED 22,000-35,000, Mohammed bin Zayed City). These nurseries are ADEK approved and offer quality education at lower fees. Trade-offs: Located further from city center, fewer premium facilities, larger class sizes (12-15 kids vs 8-10 in premium).
