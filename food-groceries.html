24-Hour Grocery Stores Abu Dhabi

When you need emergency grocery shopping in Abu Dhabi, these 24-hour grocery stores are your best option. Open around the clock, these supermarkets ensure you never run out of essentials.

Choithrams - Premium 24-Hour Chain

  • Location 1: King Khalid Bin Abdul Aziz Street, Al Khalidiya
  • Phone: +971 2 665 7009
  • Location 2: Corniche Road, near Oryx Hotel
  • Phone: +971 2 681 6930
  • Hours: 24/7 at main branches
  • Why Choose: Always open, fresh bakery, international brands
  • Best For: Emergency shopping, late-night needs
  • Average Bill: AED 200-500
  • Online: Delivery available through website

Abela Supermarket - 24-Hour Organic & Vegan Options

  • Address: Ground Floor, Bin Butti Building, Al Khalidiya
  • Phone: +971 2 667 4675
  • Hours: 24/7
  • Why Choose: Organic products, vegan options, always open
  • Best For: Health-conscious families, emergency shopping
  • Average Bill: AED 150-400
  • Special: Large selection of organic and specialty diet foods

Al Madina Hypermarket - 24-Hour Budget Option

  • Main Location: 42nd Street, Mohamed Bin Zayed City
  • Phone: +971 2 555 0033
  • Other Branches: Al Raha, Al Mafraq, Musaffah
  • Hours: 24/7
  • Why Choose: Budget-friendly, electronics section
  • Best For: Late-night shopping, household items
  • Average Bill: AED 120-350

Fresh Markets Abu Dhabi – Best Prices

For the freshest produce at wholesale prices, Abu Dhabi's traditional markets are unbeatable. These fresh markets Abu Dhabi offer significant savings on fruits, vegetables, fish, and spices.

Mina Fruit & Vegetable Market - Best Prices for Fresh Produce

  • Location: Zayed Port, Al Mina district
  • Phone: +971 2 673 2222
  • Hours: 5 AM – 12 PM daily (best selection before 9 AM)
  • Why Visit: Wholesale prices, freshest selection, direct from docks
  • Best For: Weekly fruit and vegetable shopping
  • Average Savings: 40-60% compared to hypermarkets
  • Parking: Available but crowded on weekends
  • Tip: Bring cash, bargaining expected

Abu Dhabi Fish Market - Fresh Daily Catch

  • Location: Zayed Port, adjacent to vegetable market
  • Phone: +971 2 673 3333
  • Hours: 6 AM – 12 PM daily
  • Best Selection: Early morning (6-8 AM)
  • Popular Fish: Hammour, kingfish, shrimp, crab
  • Average Prices: AED 25-45 per kg (much cheaper than supermarkets)
  • Services: Cleaning and filleting available
  • Tip: Bring cooler bag, prices negotiable
