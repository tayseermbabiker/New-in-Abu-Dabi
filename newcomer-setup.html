Neighborhood Setup Guide

Central Abu Dhabi (City Center)

  • Best for: Walking to work, restaurants, nightlife
  • Furniture stores: Homes R Us (Airport Road)
  • Internet setup: Usually fastest (existing infrastructure)
  • Banking: All major banks have branches
  • Tip: Higher rent but everything walkable

Al Reem Island

  • Best for: Modern apartments, family-friendly
  • Furniture delivery: All stores deliver here
  • Internet: Excellent fiber coverage
  • Banking: Shams Boutik Mall has all major banks
  • Tip: Plan for bridge traffic during rush hours

Khalifa City A/B

  • Best for: Spacious homes, family communities
  • Shopping: City Center Al Forsan for everything
  • Furniture: Good access to all major stores
  • Internet: Reliable, newer infrastructure
  • Banking: Local branches in most areas

Yas Island

  • Best for: Entertainment, theme parks nearby
  • Shopping: Yas Mall for all services
  • Furniture: IKEA on-site, easy delivery
  • Internet: Excellent coverage
  • Banking: All banks represented in Yas Mall
