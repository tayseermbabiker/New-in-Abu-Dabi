Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best SIM card for newcomers in Abu Dhabi 2026?

For budget users, Etisalat Visitor Line starts at AED 49 (2GB + 30 mins, 28 days). For heavy data users, Virgin Mobile offers 21GB + calls for AED 150 (7 days). Du has tourist SIMs from AED 49 and monthly plans from AED 99. All three providers offer eSIM options. Get your SIM at Abu Dhabi Airport arrivals hall (24/7) for immediate use -- you only need your passport.

Where can I buy affordable furniture in Abu Dhabi?

For new furniture: IKEA Yas Mall (delivery AED 39-99, assembly AED 50-200), Pan Emirates in Mussafah (AED 50-150 delivery, installment plans), and Homes R Us on Airport Road. For used furniture: Mussafah Used Market (open 7 AM-7 PM, bargaining expected) and Dubizzle.com for online deals. Budget tip: Start at 50% of asking price at the used market and bring a pickup truck or arrange delivery for AED 100+.

How do I open a bank account in Abu Dhabi as a newcomer?

For employees: FAB (min salary AED 5,000), ADCB or Emirates NBD (min salary AED 3,000). You need Emirates ID, salary certificate, and NOC from employer. Account fees are AED 25/month (often waived with balance). For freelancers: Liv. by Emirates NBD (AED 15/month, needs Emirates ID + UAE number only) or Mashreq Neo (AED 0 with minimum balance). Visit 8 AM-2 PM for shorter queues. Bring original documents plus copies. Debit card is usually ready same day.

How much money do I need for the first month in Abu Dhabi?

Estimated first month costs (single person): Accommodation deposit AED 2,000-4,000 (refundable), rent AED 2,000-4,000, utilities setup AED 500-1,000, groceries AED 800-1,200, transport AED 300-500, miscellaneous AED 500. Total: AED 6,100-11,200. Family of 3: Add AED 3,000-5,000. Pro tip: Your employer may advance salary or provide temporary accommodation -- ask before arriving.

What should I do in my first week in Abu Dhabi?

Priority checklist: Day 1-2: Get SIM card (Etisalat/du, AED 49-125 at airport or mall), basic groceries and transport setup. Day 3-5: Apply for internet connection (AED 299-329/month), open bank account, arrange basic furniture. Day 5-7: Assembly and installation services, neighborhood exploration. Week 2: Internet installation, final furniture, join local groups and social activities. This guide provides complete step-by-step instructions for each task with verified 2026 prices.
