Frequently Asked Questions

How much does curtain stitching cost in Abu Dhabi?

Curtain stitching prices in Abu Dhabi range from AED 15-25/meter for basic stitching to AED 30-50/meter with lining. Bedsheet tailoring costs AED 25-50 per sheet. Simple alterations like hemming cost AED 15-30. Same-day service is available for simple alterations. Mussafah and Electra Street have the most competitive prices. Always ask for samples before committing to large orders.

Where can I find budget tailors in Abu Dhabi?

Budget tailor locations in Abu Dhabi: Mussafah Industrial Area (lowest prices, many shops clustered), Electra Street (medium prices, convenient), Al Wahda/Hamdan Street (competitive pricing). For traditional clothing, Zayed First Street has specialized kandura and abaya tailors. Best time to visit is weekday mornings when it is less crowded. Bring fabric samples or photos of designs you want.

What is the best place for custom curtains in Abu Dhabi?

The best places for custom curtains in Abu Dhabi are: Electra Street Curtain Cluster (AED 20-45/meter, 1-2 day turnaround, multiple shops for price comparison), Allure Fine Tailoring at Madinat Zayed Shopping Centre (AED 20-50/meter with lining, blackout and thermal options), and Al Noor Curtains (AED 25-60/meter with free home measurement and installation included). For the cheapest fabric, buy from Al Zayani Market behind Marina Mall at AED 5-20/meter wholesale.

How much does a custom kandura or abaya cost in Abu Dhabi?

Custom kandura tailoring in Abu Dhabi costs AED 80-150, with a 2-3 day turnaround. Al Thawb Al Watani on Electra Street is a popular specialist. Women's custom abayas range from AED 120-300 at Zayed First Street tailors. General custom tailoring costs AED 150-400 depending on the complexity of the design. Bring your own fabric from wholesale markets like Al Zayani to save 30-50% on total cost.

Can I get same-day alterations in Abu Dhabi?

Yes, many tailors in Abu Dhabi offer same-day alterations for simple jobs like hemming (AED 15-30) and taking in or letting out (AED 20-40). Electra Street and Madinat Zayed area tailors are the most convenient for quick alterations. For more complex custom work like curtains or traditional clothing, expect 1-5 days depending on the order size and complexity. Visit in the morning for the fastest turnaround.
