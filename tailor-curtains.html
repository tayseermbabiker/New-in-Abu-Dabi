Frequently Asked Questions About Tailors in Abu Dhabi

How much does curtain stitching cost in Abu Dhabi?

Curtain stitching prices: AED 15-25/meter (basic), AED 30-40/meter (with lining). Bedsheet tailoring: AED 25-50 per sheet. Alterations: Hemming AED 15-30, taking in/out AED 20-40. Same-day service available for simple alterations. Mussafah and Electra Street have the most competitive prices. Always ask for samples before committing to large orders.

Where can I find budget tailors in Abu Dhabi?

Budget tailor locations: Mussafah Industrial Area (lowest prices, many shops clustered), Electra Street (medium prices, convenient), Al Wahda/Hamdaan Street (competitive pricing). For traditional clothing: Zayed First Street has specialized kandura/abaya tailors. Best time to visit: weekday mornings (less crowded). Bring fabric samples or photos of designs you want.
