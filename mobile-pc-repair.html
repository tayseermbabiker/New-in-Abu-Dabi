Smart Questions to Ask Before Repair

Quality & Warranty Questions

  • Do you use original (OEM) parts?
  • How long is your warranty (3-12 months)?
  • What exactly does the warranty cover?
  • Can I see a sample of the replacement part?

Service & Convenience Questions

  • Is there a pickup/delivery option?
  • How long will the repair take?
  • Do you accept cash or card?
  • Can you provide a written estimate?

Important Cautions & Red Flags

General Safety Tips:

  • Back up your data before repair

  • Get a receipt with repair details and promised completion date

Best Practices for Device Repair

Before You Go

  • Research the shop online - check Google reviews and ratings
  • Call ahead to confirm they can fix your specific device model
  • Ask about part availability and estimated repair time
  • Backup your data and remove personal information

During the Visit

  • Ask to see the replacement part before installation
  • Get a written estimate with labor and parts breakdown
  • Understand warranty terms - what's covered and for how long
  • Don't agree to additional work without new estimates

After Repair

  • Test all functions before leaving the shop
  • Keep your receipt and warranty information safe
  • Take photos of the repair work for your records
  • Leave an honest review to help other customers

Remember: Quality repairs may cost more upfront but save money long-term. Choose reputable shops with good reviews, proper warranties, and transparent pricing. When in doubt, get a second opinion for expensive repairs.
