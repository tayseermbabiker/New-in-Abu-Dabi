Frequently Asked Questions

Are there free Quran memorization classes in Abu Dhabi?

Yes. Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque offers free weekend family Quran classes in Arabic, English, French and German. Al Bateen Center for Holy Quran Memorisation and Hamel Alghaith Institute also offer free or low-cost programs. Registration usually opens before Ramadan and in September. Requirements: Bring Emirates ID or residency proof. Small fees may apply for books and materials (AED 50-100).

Where can I buy Islamic books in Abu Dhabi?

Dar Al Hikmah Islamic Bookstore on Electra Street has the largest selection: Quran translations, Hadith books, Islamic history, children's books in Arabic, English, Urdu, Bengali and Malayalam. Prices: Quran AED 15-150 depending on quality, Islamic books AED 20-200. Ibn Al Qayyim Islamic Records and Bookshop in Al Manaseer area is another top choice. Most mosques also have small bookshops with basic Islamic texts. Online: dar-us-salam.com delivers to the UAE.

How much do Quran classes cost in Abu Dhabi in 2026?

Quran class costs in Abu Dhabi range widely in 2026. Community programs at mosques cost AED 300-600 per month. Professional tahfiz centers like Al Falah charge AED 800-1,200 per month for full-time Hifz programs. Hamel Khadem Al Ghaith Institute charges AED 600-1,000 per month with small class sizes. Private Quran tutoring runs AED 150-300 per hour. Online programs are available from AED 200-500 per month. Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque programs are free.

What are the best Islamic centers and mosques for Quran study in Abu Dhabi?

The top Islamic centers for Quran study in Abu Dhabi include Al Falah Center for Quran Memorization (full-time Hifz and evening Tajweed, ages 5+), Hamel Khadem Al Ghaith Institute (4.7-rated, Ijazah certification, max 8 students per class), and Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque (free multilingual programs in 4 languages). For children ages 6-16, Hamel Khadem Al Ghaith offers dedicated Hifz programs. Adults can join weekend classes at most centers. All programs teach proper Tajweed recitation alongside memorization.

Can adults enroll in Quran Hifz programs in Abu Dhabi?

Yes, most Quran centers in Abu Dhabi welcome adult learners. Hamel Khadem Al Ghaith Institute offers dedicated adult weekend classes with Ijazah certification. Al Falah Center runs evening Tajweed sessions from 4 PM to 7 PM suitable for working adults. Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque has free weekend family programs open to all ages. Private tutoring at AED 150-300 per hour is another option for adults who need flexible scheduling. Online programs (AED 200-500 per month) are also available for those who prefer to study from home.
