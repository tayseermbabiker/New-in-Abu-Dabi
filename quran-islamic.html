Frequently Asked Questions About Quran Centers in Abu Dhabi

Are there free Quran memorization classes in Abu Dhabi?

Yes! Al Bateen Center for Holy Quran Memorisation and Hamel Alghaith Institute offer free Quran classes for children and adults. Classes run throughout the year with weekend and evening options. Registration usually opens before Ramadan and in September. Requirements: Bring Emirates ID/residency proof, basic Arabic reading ability helpful but not required. Small fees may apply for books/materials (AED 50-100).

Where can I buy Islamic books in Abu Dhabi?

Ibn Al Qayyim Islamic Records & Bookshop (Al Manaseer area) has the largest selection: Quran translations, Hadith books, Islamic history, children's books. Prices: Quran AED 15-150 depending on quality, Islamic books AED 20-200. Also available: Prayer mats, tasbeeh, CDs/DVDs. Most mosques have small bookshops with basic Islamic texts. Online: dar-us-salam.com delivers to UAE.
