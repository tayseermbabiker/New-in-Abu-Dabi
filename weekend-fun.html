Frequently Asked Questions About Weekend Activities in Abu Dhabi

What are the best free activities in Abu Dhabi this weekend?

Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque (free entry), Corniche Beach (free, AED 10 parking), and Umm Al Emarat Park (AED 10 adults, AED 5 kids) offer excellent free or low-cost weekend activities. The Corniche is perfect for morning jogs, sunset walks, and has playgrounds every 500m for families.

How much does a weekend at Yas Island theme parks cost?

Single park tickets: Ferrari World AED 295 online (AED 345 gate), Yas Waterworld AED 275 online (AED 315 gate). Save with multi-park packages: 2 parks in 6 days for AED 399 (save AED 191), 3 parks for AED 499 (save AED 386), or 4 parks for AED 599 (save AED 581). Always book online to save 15%.

What's the best time to visit Louvre Abu Dhabi?

Visit Tuesday-Sunday, 10 AM - 8 PM (closed Mondays). Early mornings (10-11 AM) are less crowded. Entry: AED 60 for UAE residents, AED 75 for tourists. Kids under 13 enter free, and student discounts are available. Weekend afternoons can be busy, so arrive early for a better experience.

Are there family-friendly activities at Corniche Beach?

Yes! Corniche Beach is perfect for families with kids playgrounds every 500m, bike rentals, morning jogging tracks, and sunset walking paths. Entry is free (AED 10 parking), and it's open 24/7. Perfect for morning jogs, sunset walks, and weekend picnics.

What are the best weekend activities for hot summer days?

Indoor attractions are best for summer: Ferrari World (world's fastest roller coaster), Louvre Abu Dhabi (art & culture), Yas Waterworld (water fun with indoor areas), and malls with entertainment zones. Visit outdoor attractions early morning (8-11 AM) or evening (after 5 PM) during summer.
