Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best things to do in Abu Dhabi this weekend?

Abu Dhabi offers 13 top weekend attractions in 2026. Free options include Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque, Corniche Beach, Al Bateen Beach, and Eastern Mangroves Walk. For paid entertainment, visit Yas Island theme parks (Ferrari World from AED 295, Warner Bros World, Yas Waterworld, SeaWorld) or explore Louvre Abu Dhabi (AED 60 for residents). Book online to save 15% on theme park tickets.

What are the best free activities in Abu Dhabi this weekend?

The 5 best free weekend activities in Abu Dhabi are: Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque (free entry with guided tours at 10 AM, 11 AM, 5 PM), Corniche Beach (free entry, AED 10 parking, playgrounds every 500m), Al Bateen Beach (completely free with free street parking), Eastern Mangroves Walk (free eco-tourism and nature walks), and the public areas of Qasr Al Hosn surroundings. Umm Al Emarat Park is also very affordable at AED 10 for adults.

How much does a weekend at Yas Island theme parks cost?

Single park tickets: Ferrari World AED 295 online (AED 345 gate), Yas Waterworld AED 275 online (AED 315 gate), Warner Bros World AED 295 online (AED 345 gate), SeaWorld AED 265 online (AED 305 gate). Save with multi-park packages: 2 parks in 6 days for AED 399 (save AED 191), 3 parks for AED 499 (save AED 386), or 4 parks for AED 599 (save AED 581). Always book online to save 15%.

What are the best weekend activities for hot summer days in Abu Dhabi?

Indoor attractions are best for Abu Dhabi summers: Warner Bros World (fully air-conditioned indoor theme park, AED 295), Ferrari World (indoor with world's fastest roller coaster), Louvre Abu Dhabi (world-class art museum, AED 60 residents), National Aquarium at Al Qana (AED 105 adults), and SeaWorld (AED 265 online). For outdoor activities, visit early morning (8-11 AM) or evening (after 5 PM). Yas Waterworld is ideal for cooling off during hot days.

What is there to do in Abu Dhabi on a weekend with family?

Abu Dhabi has excellent family weekend options. Free: Corniche Beach has kids playgrounds every 500m and bike rentals; Sheikh Zayed Mosque offers free guided tours. Budget-friendly: Umm Al Emarat Park (AED 10 adults, AED 5 kids) has a splash pad and evening events, with ladies-only Wednesdays after 3 PM. Theme parks: Warner Bros World is fully air-conditioned and suits all ages (AED 295). Educational: Louvre Abu Dhabi (kids under 13 free) and National Aquarium (AED 85 kids) combine fun with learning.
