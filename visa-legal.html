Employment Visa & Work Permits

New Employment Visa Process

Step 1: Work Permit (by employer)

  • MOHRE approval required
  • Labor contract preparation
  • Cost: AED 300-600
  • Time: 2-3 working days

Step 2: Entry Permit

  • After work permit approval
  • Cost: AED 500-800
  • Valid: 60 days for entry

Step 3: Medical Fitness

  • After arrival in UAE
  • Cost: AED 150-300
  • Takes: 1-2 days

Step 4: Residence Visa

  • After medical clearance
  • Cost: AED 1,200 (2 years) or AED 2,200 (3 years)
  • Emirates ID included

Employment Visa Renewal Penalties

  • Before expiry: No penalties
  • 1-30 days late: AED 200 daily fine
  • 30+ days late: AED 200 daily + AED 1,000 penalty
  • 6 months overdue: Cancellation and deportation risk

Money-Saving Tips & Shortcuts

Cost Optimization

  • Group applications: Family visas processed together save AED 100-200
  • Timing: Apply renewals 60 days before expiry (no rush fees)
  • Documentation: Keep all originals organized to avoid re-attestation
  • Medical packages: Some centers offer family medical packages

Time-Saving Tips

  • Early morning visits: Less crowded, faster service
  • Online pre-application: Complete forms online before visiting
  • Document preparation: Have all papers ready and organized
  • Peak times to avoid: Thursday afternoons, Sunday mornings

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Emirates ID cost in Abu Dhabi in 2026?

Emirates ID total cost: AED 170-190. Breakdown: Government fee AED 100, typing center service AED 50-70, photos (if needed) AED 20. Processing takes 5-10 working days. You can apply online at ICP website or through any approved typing center.

What is the difference between Tasheel Abu Dhabi and typing centers?

Tasheel centers are official MOHRE (Ministry of Human Resources) authorized centers for employment visa services only: work permits, labor contracts, employment disputes. Typing centers handle all other services: Emirates ID, family visas, translations, document attestation. For work-related visas, go to Tasheel. For family/personal documents, use typing centers.

How much does family visa sponsorship cost in Abu Dhabi?

Family of 4 total cost: ~AED 6,500. Per person: Spouse AED 1,670 (visa AED 1,200 + medical AED 300 + Emirates ID AED 170), Child AED 1,620 (visa AED 1,200 + medical AED 250 + Emirates ID AED 170). Requirements: minimum salary AED 4,000 + accommodation, attested marriage/birth certificates, tenancy contract, medical insurance.

What happens if my visa expires in Abu Dhabi?

Overstay penalties: 1-30 days late = AED 200 daily fine, 30+ days = AED 200 daily + AED 1,000 penalty, 6 months overdue = cancellation and deportation risk. Visit Tasheel center immediately if your visa is expiring. Call MOHRE hotline: +971 600 590 000 for urgent help. Renew 60 days before expiry to avoid penalties.

Where can I get free legal consultation in Abu Dhabi?

Free legal help: Abu Dhabi Legal Affairs Department (+971 2 813 4444, 8 AM - 2:30 PM Sun-Thu) provides basic legal advice and document review for UAE residents. Labor Disputes Hotline: 800-665 (24/7) for employment-related complaints in Arabic, English, Hindi, Urdu. Your embassy can also provide free legal consultation for nationals.
