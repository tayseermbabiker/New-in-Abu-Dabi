Visa & Legal Help

Need help with visas, Emirates ID, or legal paperwork in Abu Dhabi? This guide covers trusted typing centers or Tasheel branches, and where to go for family sponsorship, translations, and pro bono legal support.

Emergency Visa Situations

Visa Expiring Soon:

  • Visit Tasheel center ASAP (AED 500-1,000 penalty fees)
  • Call: +971 600 590 000 (MOHRE hotline)

Lost Passport with Visa:

  • Report to police first (get report copy)
  • Visit your embassy immediately
  • Contact Tasheel for visa transfer process

Employment Issues:

  • Labor complaint hotline: 800-665
  • Free legal consultation: Abu Dhabi Legal Affairs
  • Emergency: Contact your embassy

Employment Visa & Work Permits

New Employment Visa Process:

Step 1: Work Permit (by employer)

  • MOHRE approval required
  • Labor contract preparation
  • Cost: AED 300-600
  • Time: 2-3 working days

Step 2: Entry Permit

  • After work permit approval
  • Cost: AED 500-800
  • Valid: 60 days for entry

Step 3: Medical Fitness

  • After arrival in UAE
  • Cost: AED 150-300
  • Takes: 1-2 days

Step 4: Residence Visa

  • After medical clearance
  • Cost: AED 1,200 (2 years) or AED 2,200 (3 years)
  • Emirates ID included

Employment Visa Renewal:

  • Before expiry: No penalties
  • 1-30 days late: AED 200 daily fine
  • 30+ days late: AED 200 daily + AED 1,000 penalty
  • 6 months overdue: Cancellation and deportation risk

Money-Saving Tips & Shortcuts

Cost Optimization:

  • Group applications: Family visas processed together save AED 100-200
  • Timing: Apply renewals 60 days before expiry (no rush fees)
  • Documentation: Keep all originals organized to avoid re-attestation
  • Medical packages: Some centers offer family medical packages

Time-Saving Tips:

  • Early morning visits: Less crowded, faster service
  • Online pre-application: Complete forms online before visiting
  • Document preparation: Have all papers ready and organized
  • Peak times to avoid: Thursday afternoons, Sunday mornings

Common Mistakes to Avoid:

  • Waiting until visa expires (expensive penalties)
  • Not keeping original attested documents safe
  • Using unauthorized typing centers (delays and problems)
  • Not updating address changes with authorities
