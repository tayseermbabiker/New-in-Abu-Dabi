Public Transport System

DoT Abu Dhabi Bus Network

Hafilat Card System

📍 View on Google Maps

  • Where to get: Bus stations, Metro stations, Spinneys, Union Co-op
  • Cost: AED 25 (AED 19 balance + AED 6 card fee)
  • Top-up: Online, machines, or at counters
  • Zones: City (AED 2), Suburb (AED 4), Intercity (AED 25)
  • Validity: 3 years from last use

Main Bus Routes

  • Route 54: Airport → Marina Mall → ADNOC HQ
  • Route 5: Al Wahda Mall → Corniche → Marina Mall
  • Route 32: Khalifa City → Abu Dhabi Mall
  • Route X90: Abu Dhabi → Dubai (Express)
  • Frequency: 10-15 minutes peak, 20-30 minutes off-peak

Darb App (Real-time Tracking)

  • Download: App Store/Google Play
  • Features: Live bus locations, route planning, arrival times
  • Journey planner: Enter start/end points for best route
  • Hafilat integration: Check card balance and top-up

Driving License Conversion

Eligibility by Country (Direct Conversion)

GCC Countries (No Test Required)

  • Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Qatar, Oman, Bahrain
  • Process: Direct exchange same day
  • Cost: AED 200 + admin fees

Direct Conversion Countries

  • USA, Canada, UK, Ireland, Australia, New Zealand
  • EU: Germany, France, Italy, Netherlands, Belgium, etc.
  • Asia: Japan, South Korea, Singapore
  • Requirements: Eye test + document verification only

Test Required Countries

  • India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Philippines, Egypt, etc.
  • Process: Theory test + road test required
  • Option: Join driving school (recommended)

License Conversion Process (Step by Step)

Step 1: Document Preparation

  • Original driving license (valid minimum 6 months)
  • Certified translation (if not in English/Arabic)
  • Passport + UAE residence visa
  • Emirates ID
  • No objection certificate (NOC) from sponsor/employer

Step 2: Eye Test

📍 View on Google Maps

  • Approved centers: Al Mafraq Hospital, Sheikh Khalifa Medical City
  • Cost: AED 50-100
  • Time: 15-30 minutes
  • Validity: 3 months

Step 3: Application Submission

  • Location: Abu Dhabi Police Traffic HQ (Muroor Road)
  • Hours: 7:30 AM - 3:30 PM (Sunday-Thursday)
  • Processing time: Same day (direct conversion)
  • Photo: Taken at the center

Frequently Asked Questions About Transport & Driving in Abu Dhabi

How much does it cost to convert a driving license in Abu Dhabi?

Direct conversion (USA, Canada, UK, EU, GCC): AED 690-770 total. Breakdown: Application AED 120, license issuance AED 200, traffic file AED 320, eye test AED 50-100, translation (if needed) AED 100-150. With tests required (India, Pakistan, Philippines): AED 970-1,050 including theory test AED 80 and road test AED 200. Processing same day for direct conversion.

How does the Hafilat public transport card work?

Hafilat card costs AED 25 (AED 19 balance + AED 6 card fee). Buy at bus stations, Metro stations, Spinneys, or Union Co-op. Fares: City AED 2, Suburb AED 4, Intercity AED 25. Top-up online, at machines, or counters. Card valid 3 years from last use. Use Darb app to track buses in real-time and plan routes.

Can I convert my license from India/Pakistan/Philippines directly?

No, licenses from India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Philippines, and Egypt require both theory test and road test. You must pass both tests or join a driving school. Driving schools cost AED 1,800-4,200 for complete packages including theory lessons, practical driving, and test fees. Duration: 4-8 weeks. Emirates Driving Institute and Al Habtoor Driving Center offer fast-track courses.

Is it better to buy a car or use public transport in Abu Dhabi?

Depends on your needs. Public transport + taxi: AED 2,425-3,625 for 6 months (cheapest option). Used car: AED 8,300-9,500 for 6 months including purchase. Summer months (June-August): Car almost necessary due to extreme heat. Families: Car more convenient. Work location: Industrial areas poorly served by buses. First 3 months strategy: Use public transport to learn the city, then buy car if needed.

How much does car registration and insurance cost in Abu Dhabi?

First registration total: AED 2,500-5,000. Breakdown: Registration fee AED 420, number plate AED 400 (random) or AED 1,000+ (choice), vehicle inspection AED 170, comprehensive insurance AED 1,500-4,000/year. Best insurance: AXA (AED 1,800-3,500/year), ADNIC (AED 1,500-3,000/year), ADIB Takaful (AED 1,400-2,800/year). Requirements: valid UAE license, Emirates ID, salary certificate (minimum AED 4,000/month).
