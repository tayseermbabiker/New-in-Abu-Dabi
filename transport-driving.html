Driving License Conversion

Eligibility by Country (Direct Conversion)

GCC Countries (No Test Required)

  • Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Qatar, Oman, Bahrain
  • Process: Direct exchange same day
  • Cost: AED 200 + admin fees

Direct Conversion Countries

  • USA, Canada, UK, Ireland, Australia, New Zealand
  • EU: Germany, France, Italy, Netherlands, Belgium, etc.
  • Asia: Japan, South Korea, Singapore
  • Requirements: Eye test + document verification only

Test Required Countries

  • India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Philippines, Egypt, etc.
  • Process: Theory test + road test required
  • Option: Join driving school (recommended)

License Conversion Process (Step by Step)

Step 1: Document Preparation

  • Original driving license (valid minimum 6 months)
  • Certified translation (if not in English/Arabic)
  • Passport + UAE residence visa
  • Emirates ID
  • No objection certificate (NOC) from sponsor/employer

Step 2: Eye Test

üìç View on Google Maps

  • Approved centers: Al Mafraq Hospital, Sheikh Khalifa Medical City
  • Cost: AED 50-100
  • Time: 15-30 minutes
  • Validity: 3 months

Step 3: Application Submission

  • Location: Abu Dhabi Police Traffic HQ (Muroor Road)
  • Hours: 7:30 AM - 3:30 PM (Sunday-Thursday)
  • Processing time: Same day (direct conversion)
  • Photo: Taken at the center

Vehicle Registration & Insurance

Car Purchase & Registration Process

Required Documents for Registration

  • Valid UAE driving license
  • Emirates ID + passport copy
  • Salary certificate (minimum AED 4,000/month)
  • Vehicle purchase invoice
  • Insurance certificate (comprehensive recommended)
  • Vehicle inspection certificate (Fahs)

Registration Costs Breakdown

  • Registration fee: AED 420
  • Number plate: AED 400 (random), AED 1,000+ (choice)
  • Vehicle inspection: AED 170
  • Comprehensive insurance: AED 1,500-4,000/year
  • Total first registration: ~AED 2,500-5,000

Important Considerations

  • Summer months (June-August): Car is almost necessary due to heat
  • Family considerations: Public transport less convenient with children
  • Work location: Industrial areas poorly served by public transport
  • Resale value: Cars retain 60-70% value after 3 years in UAE
