Smart Shopping Tips

How to Get the Best Deals:

  • Visit weekday mornings (9-11 AM) for less crowds and better selection
  • Check packaging dates and inspect items before buying
  • Buy bulk non-perishables for maximum savings (40-70% off retail)
  • Compare prices on your first visit before committing to large purchases
  • Stock up during clearance sales and seasonal events
  • Avoid perishable food and high-end electronics without warranty

Frequently Asked Questions

Where are the best discount warehouses in Abu Dhabi?

The best discount warehouses in Abu Dhabi are concentrated in Mussafah Industrial Area and along Electra Street. Day To Day on Electra Street is the top choice for AED 1-20 bargains, while Mussafah has larger warehouse outlets like Al Fan Al Emarati for clothing and Wear Mart for uniforms.

What is Deal Price Warehouse Abu Dhabi?

Deal Price Warehouse is a popular discount center in the Mussafah area of Abu Dhabi, known for offering household goods, electronics accessories, and clothing at heavily reduced prices. It is one of several warehouse-style outlets in the Mussafah Industrial zone where you can save 50-80% compared to mall retail prices.

Are there liquidation stores near Mussafah?

Yes, Mussafah is Abu Dhabi's main discount center hub with multiple liquidation stores and warehouse outlets. The area near Dalma Mall has several discount warehouses selling overstock clothing, electronics, and household goods. The discount center in Mussafah typically offers 40-70% off regular retail prices.

What can you buy at warehouse outlet stores in Abu Dhabi?

Abu Dhabi's warehouse stores sell household goods (kitchenware, cleaning supplies, storage), electronics accessories, toys, clothing, bedding, furniture, dry groceries, and stationery. Best deals are on bulk household items and seasonal clearance. Always inspect items and check dates before purchasing.

How much can you save at discount stores in Abu Dhabi?

You can save 40-70% on household items, 30-50% on clothing, and 50-60% on toys at discount stores in Abu Dhabi. For example, a kitchenware set that costs AED 150 at retail sells for AED 60 at Day To Day. A family can save AED 500-1,000 monthly on household shopping by using discount warehouse stores.
