Frequently Asked Questions

How much does gym membership cost in Abu Dhabi?

Budget gyms: AED 200-450/month (GymNation AED 279-350, California Gym AED 200), Premium gyms: AED 500-900/month (Fitness First AED 279-699 varies by location), Luxury hotel gyms: AED 1,000+/month. Annual memberships save 15-25%. Look for promotions during New Year, summer, and Valentine's. Most gyms offer free trials - try before committing.

Are there ladies-only gyms in Abu Dhabi?

Yes! Ladies-only options: The Warehouse Gym Ladies (Yas Bay, complete privacy, CrossFit/boxing specialist), Vogue Fitness Yas Ladies location, Fitness First ladies-only gyms (separate facilities), Etizan Fitness Ladies Wellness Hub at Erth (exclusive section with scheduled ladies-only pool times). GymNation has dedicated ladies sections in mixed facilities.

Which gym in Abu Dhabi is open 24/7?

GymNation offers 24/7 access at all locations (Khalidiyah Mall, Mushrif Mall, Reem Island). Monthly AED 279-350 with no commitment required. Perfect for shift workers, early risers (5-7 AM), or late night exercisers (10 PM-midnight). Free trial available online. Other gyms have limited hours: Fitness First 6 AM-11 PM, Vogue Fitness 6 AM-10 PM.

What are the best wellness and spa centers in Abu Dhabi?

Top wellness and spa centers in Abu Dhabi include The Spa at St. Regis (The Corniche, marble-lined treatment rooms, traditional and modern wellness fusion) and Emirates Palace Mandarin Oriental Spa (Moroccan-inspired, ancient Eastern healing, certified Level 4 professionals). Both offer bespoke wellness programs and premium treatments. For gym-spa combos, Etizan Fitness at Erth provides Olympic-sized pool access and a dedicated Ladies Wellness Hub.

How do I choose the right gym membership in Abu Dhabi?

Start with free trials at multiple gyms before committing. Budget options (AED 200-450/month): GymNation for 24/7 access, California Gym for MMA focus. Premium options (AED 500-900/month): Fitness First for pools and group classes, Vogue Fitness for CrossFit. Compare annual vs monthly rates for 15-25% savings. Ask about corporate discounts through your employer. Consider location convenience, ladies-only requirements, and specific training needs like CrossFit, swimming, or personal training.
